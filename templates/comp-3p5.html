Relationship: Emperor Penguins and Their Prey
Emperor Penguins mainly feed on antarctic silverfish, fish, krill and squid that congregate near or under ice shelves.2

However, due to climate change and changing temperatures, the population of krill has been steadily declining. 3

On a good day, an emperor penguin will eat about 2-3kg, or double that to store up body fat. 4
