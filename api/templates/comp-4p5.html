Relationships & Interspecific Competition: Predators
As one of the only habitants of the Antarctic, there is not much competition faced by the Emperor Penguins. They are hunted by Leopard Seals and Killer Whales, which also compete for antarctic krill, fish, and squid.

Chicks and eggs are at danger of being hunted by Skuas, the Antarctic Petrel, and other large birds. 1
